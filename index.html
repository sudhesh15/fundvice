About Us

Simply put,
We build the world’s finest financial product for founders who are fledgling entrepreneurs. Hey! If you’re new to the entrepreneurial neighbourhood but with great ideas, we are here to help you settle right in. We’re perfectly aware of the stumbling blocks in your obstacle course en route to your dream company… and we’re here to sort out just that. After all, we too were “you” once upon a time. So take a few deep breaths, make yourself comfortable, approach us.

It’s a bird! It’s a plane!... It’s Superman Investment. Let’s not kid each other. FUNDVICE perfectly understands that success usually needs time and investment. Thus, we’re a proponent of ‘patient capital’, making conscious long-term investments to help companies reach their full potential and generate fuller returns (Well… this is what you wanted, isn’t it?)

We seek opportunities where we share the confidence of the starstruck entrepreneur, and in the starry ideas the entrepreneur brings in. Perhaps an immensely satisfying virtue in our trade is in the beginning of true partnerships with the founders and management teams of our partnered companies… just to ensure that we can have maximum impact on their paths to success (Yes. We never truly go away). This involvement isn’t limited to just capital provision, it evolves further beyond. We work closely with partnered companies on their business models, implement scale through further partnerships and share our experiences in developing world-class brands.
